About Us

Deveas Family Dentistry is a welcoming and compassionate dental practice dedicated to providing exceptional oral care for the entire family. Located in a modern and comfortable facility, Deveas Family Dentistry combines state-of-the-art technology with a personalized approach to ensure each patient feels at ease. The practice offers a comprehensive range of services, including preventative care, cosmetic dentistry, restorative treatments, and emergency dental care. Whether you're visiting for a routine cleaning, teeth whitening, or a more advanced procedure, their experienced and friendly team is committed to creating healthy, beautiful smiles. At Deveas Family Dentistry, patient comfort and satisfaction are the top priorities, making it the perfect choice for families seeking reliable and gentle dental care in their community.

Meet the staff

Dr. Willie DeVeas

Dr. Willie DeVeas, the head doctor at Deveas Family Dentistry, is a highly skilled and compassionate dentist dedicated to providing exceptional care to his patients. A proud graduate of the University of Maryland (UMD), Dr. DeVeas combines his advanced education with years of experience to create healthy, confident smiles. Known for his gentle approach and commitment to patient comfort, he ensures every visit is a positive experience. Dr. DeVeas is passionate about fostering long-term oral health for families in the community.
